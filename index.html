Privacy Policy of the Hedron App

The Hedron app does not collect personal data.

The Hedron app is a sandboxed application, which means it has only very restricted access to your computer.

  • Printing access
  • Read and write file access only to user selected files

  • No incoming network access
  • No outgoing network access
  • No camera access
  • No microphone access
  • No USB access
  • No bluetooth access
  • No access to your address book
  • No access to your location
  • No access to your calendar
